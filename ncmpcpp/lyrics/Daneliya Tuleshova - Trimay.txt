Yak tobi skazaty bez tebe ne mozhu spaty,
I misiats' po kimnati rozkynuv satyn,
Ochi tobi zav'yazhu i sertse svoie pokazhu,
Tobi pro vse rozkazhu z nastil'nykh kartyn.

Trymai mene mitsno, odnoiu rukoiu,
Tak syl'no i nizhno vbyvai mene,
Viddam meni muku, svoimy rukamy,
Moimy gubamy likui, likui, sebe.

Posered moiei khaty, na mene budesh krychaty,
Bo yak zhe tobi porvaty i v sertsi slidy, ti,
Chy chudiesh yak strashno meni u poloni,
Yal v tvoikh doloniakh, nema tak nide,
I kryla tak bazhko rozpravyty ptashtsi,
Koly bidolashku pohubyt' liubov.
